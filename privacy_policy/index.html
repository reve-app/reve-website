    Privacy Policy for "Reve" Mobile App

    Effective Date: 14 October 2023

    1. Introduction

    Welcome to "Reve". This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile application. By using "Reve", you agree to the terms and practices described in this policy.

    2. Information We Collect

    2.1. Google Authentication

    When you use "Reve" and choose to log in using Google authentication, we collect certain information from your Google account, which may include your name, email address, and profile picture. We use this information to create and manage your "Reve" account and provide a personalized experience.

    2.2. User-Provided Information

    "Reve" may also collect data you voluntarily provide, such as preferences, settings, and feedback, to enhance your app experience.

    3. How We Use Your Information

    3.1. Account Management

    We use Google-authenticated data to create and manage your "Reve" account, ensuring a secure and convenient login process.

    3.2. Personalization

    Your data is used to customize your in-app experience, providing you with content and features tailored to your preferences.

    3.3. Communications

    We may use your email address to send you important updates, notifications, and responses to your inquiries.

    4. Data Protection

    4.1. Data Security

    We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

    4.2. Third-Party Services

    While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee the security of information shared through third-party services such as Google authentication. Your use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

    5. Data Sharing

    5.1. Third Parties

    We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide you with the services and features of "Reve." These third parties are required to protect your data in compliance with this Privacy Policy.

    6. Your Choices

    6.1. Account Control

    You can modify your account settings, including revoking access to the app, through your Google account settings.

    6.2. Opting Out

    You may opt out of email communications from us at any time.

    7. Updates to this Privacy Policy

    7.1. Notification

    We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify you of significant changes through in-app notifications or by other means.

    8. Contact Us

    By using "Reve", you agree to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy. Please review this policy periodically for updates. Your continued use of the app constitutes your acceptance of any changes made to this policy.
